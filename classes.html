What class is the best for me?

Pur classes are designed to anyone could find a suitable class to start with. These classes cover fintess levels from absolute beginner of any age to advanced fintess level of high endurance. At your first session we would discuss with you what classes we would recommend to fit best of your goals.

How to book classes?

All classes must be pre-booked at least 24 hours in advance. We have limited availability which varies from depends of the type of the class. To book a space please phone us on 01764 456 789 or click on the book a class button. Please note that only existing members with pre-registered email address will be able to book. For a copy of our schedule click on the download sign.
